Why Choose Jairo’s Flooring?

Local Experience

With years of work in Seattle and the Northwest area, we understand the needs of the humid climate. We select materials like waterproof vinyl and engineered wood that withstand moisture and suit your style.

Quality & Warranty

We use high‑quality materials that combine beauty and durability. Our installations are backed by license and insurance, and we offer warranties for your peace of mind.

Personalized Attention

We listen to your ideas and needs to offer solutions tailored to you. We provide free advice, measurement and a no‑obligation estimate.

Our Process

  1. Free Consultation: We talk with you to understand your tastes, needs and budget.
  2. Visit & Measurement: We schedule a visit to take precise measurements and show you material samples.
  3. Detailed Estimate: We send you a clear, transparent proposal with all available options.
  4. Professional Installation: Our team installs your floor with care and cleanliness, respecting your home.
  5. Review & Warranty: We check that everything is perfect and give you maintenance recommendations.
